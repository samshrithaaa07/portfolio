My Secret Red Sauce Pasta Recipe

This red sauce pasta recipe 🍝 has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup pasta
  • 3 tomatoes and 2 onions
  • Few cashews and cloves
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta and keep it aside and then Slice the tomatoes, onions, and garlic
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add all chopped veggies
  3. Grind into a smooth paste and add salt, pepper, and red chili powder to taste
  4. Mix in the pasta and cook for 2-3 mins
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!🍽✨
